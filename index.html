Harry Mackin is a professional writer, specializing in board games, media criticism, and marketing.


After the success of his board game Décorum, Harry was hired to write for several other games, including Sagrada: Artisans, Fog of Love: Love on Lockdown, and Skyrockets.


Outside of his day job as a marketing writer, Harry has written freelance media criticism for Vice Games, Polygon, Kotaku, and more.


Harry’s film review podcast, Trylove, has over 300 episodes and posts new episodes weekly. His podcast Stoop Kidz, an episode-by-episode analysis of the 90's Nickelodeon show Hey Arnold!, hosted the show’s creator for interviews multiple times.
