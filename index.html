Harry Mackin is a writer for board games, videogames, and media criticism.

For Floodgate Games, Harry designed and wrote for his own game Décorum, and later wrote for Sagrada: Artisans, and Fog of Love: Love on Lockdown.

Harry’s videogame and media criticism has been published at Vice Games, Vox's Polygon, Gawker's Kotaku, and more.

In his free time, Harry hosts two podcasts. Trylove, a film review podcast for movies shown at the Trylon Cinema. And Stoop Kidz, an episode by episode analysis of the show Hey Arnold.

If you’d like to read some of Harry’s creative writing, please reach out for samples at mackinharry@gmail.com.
